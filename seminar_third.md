## Seminar1

***

Use ** or __ for bold

**bold**

Use * or _ for italics

_italics_

Use * for unnumbered list
* one
* two 
* three

Use 
1. one
2. two

for numbered list

Use `` for highlight

``light``

Use # to change title size

# size
## size
### size


Use ~~ for cross out

~~cross~~
